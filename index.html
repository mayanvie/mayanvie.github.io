Approach

How I work

Step 01

Start with context

Who's in the room. What they already carry. The questions they're too embarrassed to ask in week one.

Step 02

Sequence the week

Orientation before detail. A spine for the first days so nobody hits a wall of information in hour one.

Step 03

Write for doing

Plain-language guides and rehearsals. A lightweight check for whether you can do it yet. Slide decks alone don't get you there.

Step 04 · parallel

Keep the rails visible

Trackers, reminders, and handoffs. The boring parts that stop things from quietly falling apart.

Step 05 · parallel

Edit where people get stuck

If the same question comes up twice, that's usually the next paragraph to write. Not a reason to buy a new tool.
